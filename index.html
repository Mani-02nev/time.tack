    About Time Traker
    Your personal time & study dashboard

    Time Traker helps you:

    • Plan your daily study or work tasks with clear topics.
    • Track time spent on each skill (C, C++, HTML, CSS, JS, Python, Linux, AI, etc.).
    • See weekly and monthly trends with visual charts.
    • Review a full history of what you studied and when.
    • Export your data as CSV, JSON, or PDF.

    All data is stored locally in this browser using localStorage. It is not synced to any server and is only for personal tracking.
